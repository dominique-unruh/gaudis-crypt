                Mass-1 (full probability) lemmas #

                A program p has mass 1 at state σ iff p.wp (fun _ => 1) σ = 1, i.e., the total sub-probability mass produced by p at σ equals 1. This holds for every "real" probabilistic operation in the language (pure, get, set, uniform, …), and is preserved by >>=. The lemmas below let proofs about identical-until-bad analyses and similar mass-conservation arguments compose mass-1 facts cleanly.
