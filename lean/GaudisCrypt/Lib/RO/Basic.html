        Random oracle primitives #

        The basic actors of the random oracle framework: the RO state lens, the two query primitives (lazy_query and random_oracle_query), and their respective initialisation routines (lazy_init and random_oracle_init).

        convert and everything related to converting one to the other lives in PlonkLean.RO.Transfer. The scratch state variables used by the adversary-driven oracle loops live in PlonkLean.RO.OracleLoop.
