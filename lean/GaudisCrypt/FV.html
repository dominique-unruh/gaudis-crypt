              Properties of fv_reduce / fv_extend needed for fv_proc_instantiate. #

              These are stated as axioms for review. Once fv_reduce/fv_extend have real definitions they should become theorems. Note: the proof of fv_proc_instantiate needs no properties of fv_getter/fv_setter — they are used opaquely.
