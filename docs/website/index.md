We are developing a framework for cryptographic proofs in [Lean](https://lean-lang.org/).

At this point, the project is still in development and quite undocumented.

In case of questions, do not hesitate to contact us (Dominique Unruh, Denis Firsov).

The project is named after the **crypt** of Gaudí's [Church of Colònia Güell](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Church_of_Col%C3%B2nia_G%C3%BCell), known for its **lean**ing pillars.
